# 👥 HR Analytics Dashboard — Power BI

## 🎯 Project Overview
An end-to-end, production-ready Power BI report that gives HR managers
and business leaders a single interactive view of workforce headcount,
attrition rates, employee performance, salary distribution, and
department analytics — all in one cohesive HR analytics application.

## 📌 Business Problem
HR leaders needed a unified dashboard to monitor employee attrition,
identify departments with high turnover, analyze salary distribution
across roles and departments, track employee performance ratings,
and support data-driven hiring and retention decisions.

## 📁 Dataset
- Employee records with Age, Gender, Department, and Job Role fields
- Attrition and employment status data
- Salary and compensation information by role and department
- Performance ratings and satisfaction scores
- Date table for tenure and hiring trend calculations

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- **Visualization:** Power BI Desktop
- **DAX Measures:** Total Employees, Attrition Rate %, Active Employees,
  Avg Salary, Avg Age, Avg Tenure, Avg Performance Score
- **Data Modeling:** Star schema with relationships across
  Employee, Department, Date, and Performance tables
- **Design:** Dark enterprise theme with custom SVG backgrounds

## 📊 Dashboard Pages
| Page | Description |
|------|-------------|
| 🏠 Cover | Branded entry page with navigation button |
| 1️⃣ Workforce Overview | Total Employees, Active, Attrition Rate %, headcount by Department and Gender |
| 2️⃣ Attrition Analysis | Attrition by Department, Age Group, Job Role, and Years at Company |
| 3️⃣ Salary & Compensation | Avg Salary by Department, Role, and Gender — salary distribution chart |
| 4️⃣ Performance & Satisfaction | Performance ratings breakdown, satisfaction scores, and tenure analysis |

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Overall Attrition Rate % tracked across all departments and job roles
- Departments and age groups with highest employee turnover identified
- Salary distribution gaps highlighted across roles and gender
- Correlation between performance scores and employee retention

## 🎨 Design Highlights
- Dark enterprise theme with structured SVG page backgrounds
- Consistent slicer set: Department, Gender, Age Group, Job Role, Education
- Bookmark-based navigation between all pages
- Conditional formatting on Attrition Rate and Performance Score
- Transparent visual backgrounds over custom layout containers
